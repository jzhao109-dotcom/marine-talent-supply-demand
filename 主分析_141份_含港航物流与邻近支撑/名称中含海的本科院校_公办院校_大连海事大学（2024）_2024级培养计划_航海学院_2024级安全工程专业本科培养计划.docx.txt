一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
大连海事大学安全工程专业秉承“以安全为基础，以航运为特色”的办学理念，贯彻“宽口径、有特色、厚基础”的办学宗旨，依托学校在国内外的影响力以及在船舶安全、港口安全、海洋环境保护等领域多年来积累的教学科研优势，形成了以航运安全为背景的行业特色、以事故预防为主线的方法特色、以航运数字化为导向的技术特色。主要培养具有工科理论基础、掌握安全科学与工程的理论和技术、具备航运知识和安全管理能力的国际化航运安全人才。
安全工程专业拥有一支高质量的教学师资队伍，专任教师全部具有博士学位和海外留学经历，同时建设有安全人机工程实验室和火灾爆炸工程实验室，可为学生提供先进的工程实践环境和科研条件。此外，专业所在的航海学院建设有包括航海动态仿真和控制交通运输行业重点实验室、辽宁省航海安全保障重点实验室、交通信息工程辽宁省高校重点实验室、智能航运一体化应用技术辽宁省工程实验室在内的多个重点实验室。上述软硬件条件不仅可为本专业的学生提供多渠道、多元化的学习平台，还可为培养具有创新精神和研究能力的高素质航运安全人才提供强有力的智力支持。
随着社会的不断发展和国家“交通强国”、“海洋强国”战略的提出，安全科技、安全文化和安全意识得到进一步增强。《安全生产法》、《海上交通安全法》等法律法规的持续完善，使得国家及行业对安全工程专业的人才需求大幅增加，毕业生就业前景广阔。本专业的人才培养在面向交通、航运等领域的同时，兼顾其他相关领域。毕业生可从事安全科学研究、安全评估与咨询、安全检测与监控、安全技术研发与管理、安全监管监察、应急管理、安全教育培训等方面的工作。
The Safety Engineering major at Dalian Maritime University adheres to the educational philosophy of "safety as the foundation, shipping as the feature", and implements the educational mission of "wide-caliber, distinctive features, and thick foundation". The major relies on the influence of Dalian Maritime University at home and abroad and the scientific research advantages accumulated over the years in the fields of ship safety, port safety, marine environmental protection, etc., which has formed the industry feature with shipping safety as the background, the method feature with accident prevention as the mainline, and the technical feature with shipping digitalization as the guide. It aims at cultivating superior international shipping safety professionals, who will have the theoretical foundation of engineering, grasp the theory and technology of safety science and engineering, and possess the shipping knowledge and safety management capabilities.
The Safety Engineering major has a high-quality teaching faculty. All full-time teachers have doctoral degrees and overseas study experience. At the same time, a safety ergonomics laboratory and a fire and explosion engineering laboratory are built, which can provide students with advanced engineering practice and research environment. The Navigation College to which the major belongs has established multiple key laboratories, including Key Laboratory of Marine Simulation and Control for Ministry of Communications, Key Laboratory of Navigation Safety Guarantee of Liaoning Province, University Key Laboratory of Transportation Information Engineering in Liaoning Province, Engineering Laboratory of Application Technology for Intelligent Shipping Integration in Liaoning Province. The above facilities can not only provide a multi-channel and diversified learning platform for students of Safety Engineering major, but also provide intellectual support for cultivating highly qualified shipping safety professionals with innovative spirit and research capabilities.
With the continuous development of science and technology, and the initiated national strategy to build up China into a country with great transport strength and a strong maritime nation, the safety awareness in the society has been greatly raised. The continuous refinement of laws and regulations such as the "Safety Production Law" and the "Maritime Traffic Safety Law" has greatly increased the demand for safety engineering professionals. The graduates from Safety Engineering major have broad employment prospects. The program is mainly designed to serve transportation and shipping industry, with consideration of needs from other industries as well. Graduates of this major can engage in the fields of safety scientific research, safety consulting and training, safety technology R & D and management, safety emergency management and so forth.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业面向建设“交通强国”与“海洋强国”、推进航运业高质量发展的核心需求，致力于培养能够满足国家及行业发展所需的复合型安全工程技术与管理人才，使其掌握从事安全科学与工程类工作所需的自然科学、人文社会科学、工程技术等的基础知识和专业知识，并具备安全设计分析、安全技术管理等的基本能力，同时能够胜任以安全、数字、绿色、智能为指向的航运产业技术应用、管理与创新工作。
The major is dedicated to cultivating safety professionals who can meet the core needs of building China into a country with great transport strength and a strong maritime nation, and promoting the high-quality development of the shipping industry, so that they can master the knowledge of natural science, humanities and social sciences, engineering technology, etc., required to engage in safety science and engineering work. Furthermore, the graduates are expected to be competent in safety design and safety technology management, etc., to contribute to a safer, digitalized, and smart shipping industry.
三、毕业要求
III．Graduation Requirements
本专业毕业生要求掌握自然科学、工程基础、专业知识和社会科学知识，并能够选择与应用合适的技术及工具来发现、分析和解决复杂工程问题，具体要求如下：
Graduates of this major are required to grasp natural sciences, engineering fundamentals, expertise and social science knowledge, who can select and apply appropriate techniques and tools to discover, analyze and solve complex engineering problems. The required abilities are described in detail as follows:
工程知识：具备将数学、自然科学、工程基础和专业知识应用于安全科学与工程领域复杂工程问题的能力。
Engineering knowledge: The ability to apply mathematics, natural sciences, engineering fundamentals and expertise to complex engineering issues in the field of safety science and engineering.
问题分析：能够应用数学、自然科学和工程科学的基本原理，识别、表达、并通过文献研究分析复杂安全工程技术问题，以获得有效结论。
Problem analysis: The basic principles of mathematics, natural sciences and engineering sciences can be applied to identify, express, and analyze complex safety engineering problems through literature research to obtain effective conclusions.
设计/开发解决方案：能够设计针对复杂安全工程问题的解决方案，设计满足特定需求的工作流程，并能够在设计环节中体现创新意识，考虑社会、健康、安全、法律、文化以及环境等因素。
Design/development of solutions: Design solutions for complex safety engineering problems, design processes that meet specific needs, and be able to embody innovation awareness in the design process, taking into account social, health, safety, law, culture and environment and other factors.
研究：能够基于安全科学原理并采用安全工程科学方法对安全工程问题进行研究，包括设计安全实验、分析与解释数据，并通过信息综合得到合理有效的结论。
Investigation: The ability to study complex safety engineering problems based on safety science principles and scientific methods, including design safety experiment, analyze and explain data, to obtain reasonable and valid conclusions via information synthesis.
使用现代工具：能够针对复杂安全工程问题，开发、选择与使用恰当的技术、资源、现代工程工具和信息技术工具，并能够理解其局限性。
Modern tool usage: The ability to select and use appropriate technologies, resources, modern engineering tools and information technology tools for complex safety engineering issues, and the ability to understand its limitations.
工程与社会：能够基于安全工程相关背景知识进行合理分析，评价航运安全实践和复杂工程问题解决方案对社会、健康、安全、法律以及文化的影响，并理解应承担的责任。
Engineering and society: The ability to conduct a rational analysis based on relevant background knowledge of safety engineering, evaluate the impact of shipping safety practices and complex engineering problem solutions on society, health, safety, law and culture, and understand the corresponding responsibilities.
环境和可持续发展：能够理解和评价针对安全工程问题的工程实践对环境、社会可持续发展的影响。
Environment and sustainability: The ability to understand and evaluate the impact of engineering practices on complex safety engineering issues on environmental and social sustainability development.
职业规范：具有人文社会科学素养、社会责任感，能够在工程实践中理解并遵守工程职业道德和规范，履行责任。
Ethics: Possess humanities and social science accomplishment, social responsibility, understand and comply with engineering ethics and norms in engineering practice, to fulfill responsibilities.
个人和团队：能够在多学科背景下的团队中承担个体、团队成员以及负责人的责任。
Individual and teamwork: The ability to take responsibility as an individual, team member, and person in charge in a multidisciplinary team.
沟通：能够就安全工程问题与业界同行及社会公众进行有效沟通和交流，包括撰写报告和设计文稿、陈述发言、清晰表达或回应指令。并具备一定的国际视野，能够在跨文化背景下进行沟通和交流。
Communication: The ability to effectively communicate and discuss with industry peers and public on safety engineering issues, including writing reports and design presentations, making statement, expressing clearly, or responding to instructions. And have the international perspective, to communicate and discuss in cross-cultural background.
项目管理：理解并掌握安全管理原理与决策方法，并能在多学科环境中应用。
Project management: To understand and consolidate the principles of safety management and decision-making methods, and be able to apply them in a multidisciplinary environment.
终身学习：具有自主学习和终身学习的意识，有不断学习和适应发展的能力。
Life-long learning: Possess the consciousness of independent and lifelong learning, and the ability to continuously learn and adapt to development.
