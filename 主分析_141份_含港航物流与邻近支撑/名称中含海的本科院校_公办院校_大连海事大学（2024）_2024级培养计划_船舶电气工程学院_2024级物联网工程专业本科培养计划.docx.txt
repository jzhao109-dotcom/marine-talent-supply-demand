一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
物联网工程是一门适应性强、应用面广的工程技术学科，是以系统理论为基础，以电子技术、传感器技术、计算机技术、网络通信技术为主要工具，采集信息，传送信息，并从系统的角度分析处理各种信息的专业。它具有“感（传感）、传（通讯）、算（计算机）、控（控制）一体化，覆盖面广、集成度强、学科交叉强烈”等鲜明的特点，是新技术驱动的多学科交叉的宽口径工科专业。
Internet of Things Engineering is an engineering technical subject based on system theory, acquiring, transferring and processing various information from the viewpoint point of system, by using electric technology, sensor technology, computer technology and network communication technology. It involves distinct features of wide coverage, high integration, strong multi-discipline intersection, and deep fusion of sensing, communicating, computing and controlling, which render it a multi-discipline and wide scope engineering program driven by new technologies.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业适应国家战略性新兴产业发展需求，培养学生德、智、体、美，劳全面发展，知识、能力、素质兼备。具有较高的道德文化修养和科学素质，良好的沟通与团队协作能力，较强的社会责任感和终身学习能力；具有物联网工程领域的专业知识及较强的实践能力和创新意识。能在物联网工程（尤其是交通相关领域）从事系统设计、研发、维护和管理的高素质复合型专门人才。
This major adapts to the development needs of national strategic industries, and focus on all-round development of students, including not only knowledge but also ability and comprehensive quality. This program aims at training talents with high moral and scientific quality, good communication and teamwork skills, strong sense of social responsibility and awareness of life-time learning, along with professional knowledge in field of Internet of Things Engineering and good practical ability. Students are expected to engage in system design, research and development, maintenance and management in field of Internet of Things Engineering (especially in transportation related fields).
