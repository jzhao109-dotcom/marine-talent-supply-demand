Undergraduate Program for Mathematics and Applied Mathematics Major
一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
为了满足日益增长的数学人才的需求，我校经交通部与教育部批准于2007年设立数学与应用数学本科专业。本专业依托辽宁省重点学科“应用数学”，于2020年入选辽宁省一流本科教育示范专业。数学与应用数学专业培养掌握数学科学的基本理论与基本方法，具备运用数学知识、计算机解决实际问题的能力，受到科学研究的初步训练的高级专门人才。本专业学生修业四年，满足专业要求者授予理学学士学位。
In order to meet the growing demand for mathematics talents, Dalian Maritime University established the undergraduate major of mathematics and applied mathematics in 2007 with the approval of the Ministry of Transportations and the Ministry of Education. With the support of Liaoning Province's key discipline "Applied Mathematics", this major was selected as a first-class undergraduate education demonstration major in Liaoning Province. The mathematics and applied mathematics major trains senior professionals who master the basic theories and methods of mathematical science, have the ability to apply mathematical knowledge and computers to solve practical problems, and receive preliminary training in scientific research. Students of this major study for four years, and those who meet the major requirements are awarded a Bachelor of Science degree.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业依据“理论与实践并重，宽口径与强化基础相结合”的原则，培养具有坚实的数学基础理论知识和严密的数学逻辑思维能力，掌握数学与应用数学的基本思想方法，受到系统的科研与实践训练，能综合运用数学知识和计算机工具解决实际问题的高素质人才。本专业毕业生可在高校、科研机构、高新技术企业、金融、电信等部门从事数学教育、科学研究、图形图像及信号处理、自动控制、统计分析、信息管理、科学计算等工作。同时也可以继续深造，攻读数学或相关学科的研究生。
Based on the principle of "emphasizing both theory and practice, and combining wide caliber with strengthening foundation", this major aims to train students with solid mathematical basic theoretical knowledge and rigorous mathematical logic thinking ability, master the basic thinking methods of mathematics and applied mathematics, receive systematic scientific research and practical training, and be able to comprehensively use mathematical knowledge and computer tools to solve practical problems.  Graduates of this major can be engaged in mathematics education, scientific research, graphic image and signal processing, automatic control, statistical analysis, information management, scientific computing, and other work in universities, scientific research institutions, high-tech enterprises, finance, telecommunications, and other departments. It is also possible to pursue further studies and pursue postgraduate studies in mathematics or related disciplines.

一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
为了满足大数据时代对统计人才需求日益增长的趋势，以交通运输行业对统计人才的需求为背景，我校经交通部与教育部批准于2012年设立统计学本科专业。统计学是研究如何有效地收集、整理和分析带有随机性的数据，在此基础上对所考察的问题进行统计推断和学习，并为采取进一步的决策和行动提供理论依据及预测的学科。该专业设立的宗旨是经过四年系统的专业培养，使学生具备独立从事各领域数据的统计、分析与预测的能力。满足专业要求的学生可授予理学学士学位。
Undergraduate major of statistics in our university is authorized by Traffic Minister and Education Minister in 2012, in order to meet the increasing demand of statisticians in the big data era, especially in the transportation industry. Statistics is a subject that (1) studies how to collect, sort out and analyze random data effectively; (2) conducts further deduction or prediction for the investigated problems; (3) provides a theoretical support for further decision and action. The purpose of this major is to enable students to independently engage in statistics, analysis and prediction of data in various fields after four years of systematic professional training. All students who meet these major requirements will be conferred a Bachelor of Science degree.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业培养具有坚定民族精神、强烈社会责任感和使命感、人格健全、身心健康，并具有开阔国际视野的统计学人才。通过系统地学习统计学专业相关的基本理论、基础知识和基本实践技能，具有良好的数学素养、扎实的数据分析能力，具备较强的创新意识和更新知识的能力。本专业将在广泛而充实的通才教育的基础上，结合大数据时代对专业人才的需求，与航运、交通、物流、生物及海洋环境等有重要特色的学科相结合，开阔学生学科交叉视野，提高熟练运用计算机进行数据处理和数据分析的能力。培养适于从事统计调查、信息管理、数据分析等工作，具有较强竞争力的专门人才。
This major cultivates statisticians with strong national spirit, strong sense of social responsibility and mission, sound personality, physical and mental health, and broad international vision. By systematically learning the basic theories, basic knowledge and basic practical skills related to statistics, students have a good mathematical literacy, solid data analysis ability, and a strong sense of innovation and the ability to update knowledge. On the basis of extensive and substantial general education, combined with the demand for professionals in the era of big data, this major will be combined with important disciplines such as shipping, transportation, logistics, biology and marine environment, so as to broaden students' interdisciplinary vision and improve their ability to skillfully use computers for data processing and data analysis. Our goal is to train highly competitive professionals who are suitable for statistical investigation, information management and data analysis.

毕业要求5	了解数学与应用数学的理论前沿、应用前景和最新发展动态；具有创新意识和创新能力，了解某个应用领域，能运用所学的理论、方法和技能解决某些科研生产中的实际问题。 The students are supposed to: be familiar with the latest developments in mathematics and applied mathematics; have an innovative spirit; be familiar with a certain field of application; be able to use learned theories, methods, and techniques to solve problems in research and production.
