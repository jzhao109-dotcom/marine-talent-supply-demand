一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
大连海事大学人工智能专业于2021年2月获教育部批准设立，是学校围绕智能交通、智慧海洋、智慧法学等领域智能化发展需求重点筹建的特色专业。本专业注重基础理论人才与“人工智能+交通运输工程”复合型人才培养。在新一代人工智能基础理论、知识计算与智能算法、类脑计算与群体智能、高可信工业互联网与大数据智能、自然语言处理与跨媒体智能、自主智能与无人系统、智慧交通与智能航运等领域，本专业设置了内容丰富且具有学校特色的理论课程和创新实践课程体系。
The artificial Intelligence major of DMU was established with the approval of the Ministry of Education in February 2021. It is a characteristic major that DMU focuses on the development needs of intelligent transportation, smart ocean, and smart law and other fields. This major focuses on the cultivation of basic theoretical talents and compound talents of "artificial intelligence + transportation engineering". In the fields of basic theory of new generation artificial intelligence, knowledge computing and intelligent algorithms, brain-like computing and swarm intelligence, high-trust industrial Internet and big data intelligence, natural language processing and cross-media intelligence, autonomous intelligence and unmanned systems, intelligent transportation and intelligent shipping, this major has set up a theoretical curriculum and innovative practical course system with rich content and school characteristics.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业从人工智能专业的具体内涵出发，采用“人工智能+交通运输工程”复合型人才培养模式，培养学生具有良好人文素养、强烈的事业心、使命感及担当精神；具有创造性和批判性思维能力；具有全球化视野和终身学习能力；具有能够使用人工智能专业的基础理论和专业技能，在智能交通、智慧海洋、智能法学等交叉学科提出并解决人工智能领域挑战性问题的能力；具有能够担负人工智能相关科学技术或工程管理工作的能力；具有能够服务国家重大需求，增强交通强国和海洋强国核心竞争力的能力。最终培养学生成为交通运输工程领域特色的复合型智能创新人才。
Starting from the specific connotation of artificial intelligence major, this major adopts the compound talent training model of "artificial intelligence + transportation engineering" to cultivate students with good humanistic literacy, strong sense of professionalism and mission, spirit of responsibility, creative and critical thinking ability, global vision and lifelong learning ability; with the ability to use the basic theories and professional skills of artificial intelligence major to propose and solve challenging problems in the field of artificial intelligence in interdisciplinary disciplines such as intelligent transportation, intelligent ocean, and intelligent law; with the ability to undertake artificial intelligence-related science and technology or project management; with the ability to serve the major needs of the country and enhance the core competitiveness of transportation power and marine power. Finally, students will be trained to become compound intelligent innovative talents with characteristics in the field of transportation engineering.
