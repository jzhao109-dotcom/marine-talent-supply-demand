Undergraduate Program for Geographical Information Science Major
一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
地理信息科学专业是一门集空间信息、遥感、测量、计算机与网络技术等于一体的新兴高技术交叉学科，研究地理空间信息采集、分析、存储、显示、管理、传播、应用及地理信息流的产生、传输和转化规律，解决交通、航海、港口管理、海事管理、物流、测绘、海洋、环境监测、规划管理等领域的理论与应用问题。
Geographic information science (GIScience) is a new scientific crossing discipline related to spatial information, remote sensing, surveying, computer and network science. GIScience studies structures and computational techniques to collect, analyze, storage, visualize, manage, convey and apply spatial data, as well as the law of generation, transmission and transformation of geo-informational flow. It can be help solve theoretical and practical problems in other fields, including marine navigation, port management, maritime safety administration, physical distribution, surveying, ocean, environmental monitoring, planning, etc.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业主要面向“四个交通”、“海洋强国”战略，培养适应交通空间信息化建设需要，德智体美劳全面发展，掌握扎实的地理信息技术、交通信息化技术，熟悉国际公约和国际形势变化，能够胜任“互联网+”与“大数据”背景下的交通、航海、海洋和海事信息化建设、具有良好的人文素养、科学素养、较强实践和创新创业能力的高级应用和管理人才。
Facing the “Four transportation” and “marine power” strategy, this major mainly cultivates students to become senior application and management talents who have good humanistic and scientific literacy and strong practical, innovative and entrepreneurial abilities, can accommodate the need of traffic information construction, have all-round development in morality, intelligence, physical fitness and aesthetics, master the space information technology and traffic information technology, meanwhile be acquainted with the international convention and the changes of international situation. They are qualified to the information construction of transportation, navigation, ocean and maritime affair under the background of “Internet +”and “Big Data”.
三、毕业要求
III．Program Requirements
毕业要求1	具有正确的世界观和人生观，热爱祖国，对祖国和社会有高度的责任感；具备敬业爱岗、艰苦奋斗、热爱劳动、遵纪守法、团结合作的品质。
毕业要求2	掌握专业所需的数学、物理和计算机等自然科学和工程科学的基本原理，并能够将相关基本理论知识应用于地理实际问题。
毕业要求3	系统地掌握地理信息系统、遥感技术、卫星定位导航等空间信息的基本理论、基本知识和基本实践技能以及地理信息系统技术开发的原理和方法，了解学科应用前景和最近发展动态，具有整理、归纳、分析相关数据并得到合理有效的结论的能力。
毕业要求4	能够针对复杂地理信息及遥感问题，选择、使用恰当的资源、技术和工具进行定量研究和解决实际问题，具有地理学区域综合分析能力。
毕业要求5	掌握资料查询、文献检索及运用现代信息技术获取相关信息的基本方法；具备撰写论文、参与学术交流的能力；具备一定的国际视野，能够在跨文化背景下进行沟通和交流。
毕业要求6	具备一定的创新创业能力，具有自主学习和终身学习的意识，有不断学习和适应发展的能力。
Students have a correct world outlook and outlook on life, love the motherland, have a high sense of responsibility for the motherland and society, and have the qualities of dedication and love for work, hard work, love of labor, compliance with discipline and law, unity and cooperation.
Students can master the basic principles of natural and engineering sciences such as mathematics, physics and computers required for the major, and are able to apply relevant basic theoretical knowledge to practical geographical problems.
Students can systematically grasp the basic theories, basic knowledge and basic practical skills of spatial information such as geographic information systems, remote sensing technology, satellite positioning and navigation, as well as the principles and methods of geographic information system technology development, understand the application prospects and recent developments of the discipline, and have the ability to sort, summarize and analyze relevant data and obtain reasonable and effective conclusions.
Students are able to select and use appropriate resources, technologies and tools for quantitative research and practical problems for complex geographic fidelity and remote sensing problems, and have the ability to comprehensively analyze geographical areas.
Students can master the basic methods of data query, literature retrieval and the use of modern information technology to obtain relevant information; have the ability to write papers and participate in academic exchanges; have a certain international perspective, and be able to communicate and communicate in cross-cultural contexts.
Students have a certain ability of innovation and entrepreneurship, have the awareness of independent learning and lifelong learning, and have the ability to continuously learn and adapt to development.
