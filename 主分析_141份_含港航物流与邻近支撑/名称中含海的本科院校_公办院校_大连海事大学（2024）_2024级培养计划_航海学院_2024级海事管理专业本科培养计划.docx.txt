Undergraduate Program for Maritime Safety Administration Major
一、专业简介
I．Program Profile
1987年交通部（现为交通运输部）在我校设置的航政管理专业，是国内第一个海事类高等教育本科专业，2001年更名为海事管理。2007年被评为大连海事大学特色专业，2008年被评为大连海事大学示范专业，2009年被评为辽宁省普通高等学校示范专业，2020年被评为辽宁省普通高等学校一流本科专业。海事管理专业主要培养适应国家海事事业发展，掌握海上交通工程、航海技术、公共管理的基本理论，能够运用海事公约和法规、水上安全监督、船舶防污染管理、航海保障等知识和技能从事相关工作的专业人才。毕业生就业主要面向国家和地方海事系统，部分分布在港口、航运、物流、石化等相关领域。
As the pioneer maritime undergraduate major, Maritime Administration Major was founded by Ministry of Transport of the People’s Republic of China in 1987, renamed Maritime Safety Administration in 2001. The major was honored as one of the six characteristic majors in Dalian Maritime University (2007), the demonstration major in Dalian Maritime University (2008), demonstration major in colleges and universities of Liaoning Province (2009), and first-class undergraduate major in colleges and universities of Liaoning Province (2020) successively. The major cultivated professionals in maritime and public administration related area who had specialties of adapting to the need of national maritime development, possessing the fundamental theories and technologies for marine traffic engineering, nautical science and public management, applying the basic knowledge of maritime laws and international treaties, marine safety superintendence, environmental protection as well as navigation support. Most of the graduates are working for the national maritime systems, while the rest spreading in related field such as harbor, shipping, logistics and petrifaction.
二、培养目标
II．Program Objectives
本专业培养德、智、体、美全面发展，掌握海事公共管理理论、方法和技术，能运用海事管理的基础理论、专门知识和专业技能，具备“航行安全、水域清洁、服务交通、奉献社会”的公共意识和责任，具有创新精神、创业意识和创新创业能力，能适应社会发展要求的海事专业人才。
This program aims to cultivate the students to be fit for the need of China socialism modern construction, obtain the basic training of engineering, be able to use English and computer proficiently, master the basic theories and skills of the safety management, maritime safety administration and navigation; and train those educated to the senior talents in maritime administration with all-round development of morality, intelligence and physique.

本年度培养计划在多次校对、反复审核的过程当中，得到了各教学单位领导、教务、以及老师的大力支持和帮助。在此，对在培养计划修订过程中给予支持和帮助的各位，表示衷心的感谢。
培养计划的制定是一项繁琐的系统工作，难免会有疏漏，不足之处还望批评指正。培养计划的修订是以专业发展和知识更新为需求导向的，在执行过程中会进行细微调整。在此过程中，若产生分歧，应以教务系统为准。
