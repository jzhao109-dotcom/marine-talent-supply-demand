一、专业简介
I. Program Profile
本专业以“基础化、综合化、个性化、实践化”的专业创新型人才培养模式为办学思想，以轮机工程为基础并与自动化、信息化、智能化等现代科学技术相结合，培养学生具备轮机设计、船机修造和船舶智能运维等基础知识与应用能力。学生就业范围广，能在船舶与海洋工程、动力、工程机械、能源等相关领域从事设计、制造、安装、运行、管理、营销等工作，也可到船级社、海事管理部门以及国内同类型院校等单位从事科研、教学与管理等相关工作。
The maritime engineering major is based on the "fundamental, comprehensive, personalized and practical" professional innovative talents training mode as the school-running idea. Based on maritime engineering, and combined with modern science and technology such as automation, information, and artificial intelligence, this major will make the students obtain the basic knowledge and application ability of marine design, ship machinery repairing and building and Intelligent operation and maintenance of ship. Students in this major have a wide range of employment. Graduates can engage in design, manufacturing, service and production management in the fields of ship and ocean engineering, power, engineering machinery, energy and other fields. They can also engage in scientific research, education, management in the classification societies, maritime management departments as well as the domestic similar university and other units.
二、培养目标
II. Program Objectives
本专业培养掌握轮机工程的基础理论和专业知识，能够从事轮机的设计、制造、运维等方面工作，具备解决轮机领域复杂工程问题的能力，并在工程实践中遵守职业道德和规范，具有创新、法律、环境意识、团队合作协调能力与国际视野的高级工程技术人才。学生在毕业后5年左右，预期成长为技术专家、业务骨干或管理人才。
上述培养目标可具体表述为以下四个子项：
培养目标 1：系统地掌握数学、自然科学、工程基础和专业知识与技能，能够从事轮机的设计、制造、运维等方面工作；
培养目标 2：具备独立分析和解决复杂工程问题的基本能力，能够成长为技术专家或管理人才；
培养目标 3：具备高度的社会责任感、职业道德和创新精神，能在工程实践中考虑和评价社会、健康、安全、法律、环境及可持续发展等方面的问题；
培养目标 4：具有较强的团队协作、人际交往、组织协调和跨文化合作交流能力；具备自主和终身学习能力，不断提升自我，以适应社会经济发展的需要。
The maritime engineering major aims to cultivate high quality engineering and technical talents who can master the basic theory and professional knowledge of the marine engineering, be engaged in design, manufacturing, operations and other works about marine, have the ability to solve the complex engineering problem, abide by the professional ethics and norms in the engineering practice, and possess the awareness of innovation, legal, environmental, the ability for teamwork and coordination and the international perspective. After 5 years around, the graduates are expected to become technical experts, backbone or management talents.
The above-mentioned training objectives can be specifically expressed as the following four sub-items:
Training goal 1: Students systematically master basic knowledge and skills of mathematics and natural sciences and professional, and engage indesign, manufacturing, operations and other works about marine;
Training goal 2: Students have the basic ability to independently analyze and solve complex engineering problems and be able to become a professional and technical backbone in the field of work;
Training goal 3: Students have a high sense of social responsibility, professional ethics and innovative spirit, and be able to consider and evaluate social, health, safety, legal, environmental and sustainable development issues in engineering practice;
Training goal 4: Students have strong teamwork, interpersonal communication, organization and coordination and cross-cultural cooperation and communication skills; have the ability to autonomy and lifelong learning, and constantly improve themselves to meet the needs of social and economic development.
